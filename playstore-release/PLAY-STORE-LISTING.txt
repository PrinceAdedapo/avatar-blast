YOU are the strategist behind their greatest battle. Guide their elemental powers. Clear the corruption. Save the world.

━━ ACTION-PACKED GAMEPLAY ━━

Avatar Blast is a fast-paced match-3 puzzle game where every swap is a tactical decision:

• MATCH 3+ elemental gems to clear corruption and score points
• RACE THE CLOCK — complete each mission before time runs out
• LIMITED MOVES — think like Sokka, every swap must count
• COLLECT rare gems to unlock new nations and characters
• CHAIN CASCADES for massive combo multipliers

━━ SPECIAL BENDING POWERS ━━

Master devastating special gem combinations:

• Match 4 → LINE BLAST — A bending strike clears an entire row or column!
• L or T shape → BOMB — Toph's seismic sense, 3×3 elemental explosion!
• Match 5+ → AVATAR STATE — Korra channels all elements, eliminates a color!

Chain specials together for screen-shaking destruction.
